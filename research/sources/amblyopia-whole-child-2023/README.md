# Amblyopia and the whole child (2023)

**Citation:** "Amblyopia and the whole child." 2023.
**Access:** Open access (<https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC9998377/>).
**Verification:** From search digest.

## Scope

Review of the broad functional and developmental impact of amblyopia beyond
visual acuity.

## Key points

- Amblyopia affects up to ~**4%** of children and often persists into
  adulthood.
- Interferes with contrast sensitivity, attention, reading, eye–hand
  coordination, physical activity, and health-related quality of life.

## Learnings for this project

- Establishes amblyopia as the most common cause of functional monocular
  vision and frames the children/families content.
